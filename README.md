

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze retail sales performance and profitability using a retail dataset sourced from Kaggle.

The objective of this project was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, calculated measures, and interactive visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Purpose

The purpose of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales and profit performance  
- Identify top-performing cities and product categories  
- Track monthly sales and profit trends  
- Understand customer payment behavior  
- Support data-driven decision-making  

This dashboard helps convert transactional data into clear and actionable insights.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and visualization  
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Creating calculated measures  
- **Kaggle Retail Sales Dataset** – Data source  

---

## 🔄 Data Preparation (ETL Process)

### Extract
- Imported the retail dataset from Kaggle into Power BI.

### Transform (Power Query)
- Removed duplicate records  
- Handled missing values  
- Cleaned inconsistent text data  
- Formatted date and numeric columns  
- Corrected data types (currency, numbers, percentages)

### Load
- Loaded the cleaned dataset into Power BI for analysis and visualization.

---

## 📐 DAX Measures Created

The following measures were created to analyze performance:

- Total Sales  
- Total Orders  
- Total Quantity  
- Average Order Value (AOV)  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin %  
- Average Profit per Order  

These measures power the KPI cards and visualizations in the dashboard.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard consists of two pages:

### 1️⃣ Sales Performance

- KPI cards (Total Sales, Orders, Quantity, AOV)  
- Sales by City  
- Sales by Category  
- Revenue by Payment Method  
- Monthly Sales Trend  
- Year and Quarter filters  

### 2️⃣ Profitability Analysis

- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin %  
- Average Profit per Order  
- Profit by City  
- Profit by Category  
- Monthly Profit Trend  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Total Sales reached approximately $1.21M.  
- Overall Profit Margin is around 39%.  
- Sales and profit peak during November and December.  
- Home & Lifestyle and Fashion Accessories are top-performing categories.  
- Credit Card payments contribute the highest share of revenue.  
- Some cities generate higher revenue and profit compared to others.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory and marketing efforts before Q4 to maximize seasonal sales.  
- Focus on expanding high-performing product categories.  
- Analyze lower-performing cities to identify growth opportunities.  
- Continuously monitor profit margins to ensure sustainable growth.
